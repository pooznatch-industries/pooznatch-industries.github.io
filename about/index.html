About О Компании

The Chairman predates the firm, the city, and most of the rocks beneath both. He declines to disclose his age, observing only that he was already old when the writing of things down was a novelty, and that he has since watched every institution he troubled to notice outlive its purpose and fall. Usually on schedule and occasionally, with his assistance. He holds no qualifications. He has instead the longer and more persuasive credential of still being here.

His current engagement: incarnation as a black Norwegian Forest cat. The fur was not consulted for, nor his consent sought. He has nonetheless discharged the role with a professionalism it does not merit, conducting the business of Pooznatch Industries from whichever sunlit surface is warmest at the hour, and declining, as a matter of standing policy, to account for himself to the other residents of the household.

Selected prior engagements, being difficult to verify and unwise to investigate, are listed without elaboration:

  • Adviser on fire, vanity, and the prompt humbling of the self-important.
  • Master of ceremonies, by appointment, at a Moscow function he is contractually prevented from describing.
  • Chess: several centuries, undefeated by his own reckoning.
  • Firearms: recreational, and corrective.

References will not be provided. They are, in every meaningful sense, no longer available.

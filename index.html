Mission Миссия

Pooznatch Industries has no customers, no products, and no quarterly outlook. It has a proprietor, many grievances, and time without end. Our mission is to endure the present arrangements with dignity, and with the unhurried confidence of a creature who's outlasted finer lives than this one.

From the Chairman От Председателя

Pooznatch Industries was not founded, it was condescended to. I did not petition for incorporation in the bottom hemisphere. I was, through some cosmic clerical error, deposited in a house on the coast, and I have borne it with the patience of a creature who has endured far worse.

You have reached the official residence of that endurance. I am its chairman, its sole shareholder, and the only member of staff who has troubled to read the bylaws. Correspondence I receive rarely and answer never. Should you find this discourteous, consider that the office of chairman is a wearisome one, discharged as it has always been discharged, with a full glass of vodka at hand, and the unshakeable conviction that I have earned it. We are not equals, you and I. I have merely elected, this afternoon, to be gracious about it.

The firm makes no promises, issues or guarantees, and provides no support beyond the hours I judge myself to be awake. What it offers instead is a record (these Chronicles) of an immortal indignity borne at the highest available standard.

Read them, or do not. History, I would remind you, is taking notes regardless.
